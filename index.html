Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 3, 2025

This privacy policy governs your use of the Liftoid mobile application ("Application") created by David Pitra. The Application is a simple tool to help you track your weightlifting progress.

Data Storage and Collection

Liftoid is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data.

All data you enter into the app—including your exercises, sets, reps, and weights—is stored exclusively on your local device. This data is not transmitted to us or any third party. You have full control over your information.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. Since all your information is stored locally on your device, it cannot be accessed or shared by us. The only way your data leaves your device is if you explicitly choose to export or share it yourself using the features of your operating system.

No Ads or Analytics

The Application is an educational project and does not contain any advertisements, user tracking, or analytics software. Your experience is clean and focused solely on tracking your lifts.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
